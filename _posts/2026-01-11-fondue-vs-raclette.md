### The Internet Star: Fondue

If we let the internet decide, fondue is the undisputed champion. When the snow starts to fall here, everyone reaches for their phones to search for this friendly cozy meal.

**The Search Arena:** According to Google search data, the volume for "fondue" is typically much higher than for "raclette". Is this because we are looking for recipes, while raclette is simpler? Beware of the "fondue chinoise", which also contributes to the volume!

### The Social Star: Raclette

While fondue wins the "search war" on Google, raclette is winning our hearts. Lately, there has been a huge move toward the "experience economy": we don't just want to eat, we want a show! Scraping melted cheese off a big wheel is incredibly social and looks amazing on your phone.

**Purity of Intent:** Even though we eat 1.35 kg of raclette cheese per capita, this number is special. Why? Because while people use Gruy√®re for sandwiches or snacks, raclette cheese is almost only used for the actual meal ritual. When you buy it, you mean business!

**World Record Vibes:** In April 2025, we proved how much we love it by breaking a world record in Martigny. Nearly 5,000 people gathered to eat over 2,037 kg of cheese. The tickets were so popular they sold out in just one hour and 20 minutes!

**Decades-Long Trend**: For the past 2 decades, the search trend has been clearly moving in favor of raclette, with a search volume ratio going from over ~5 all the way down to ~2 in 2025-2026 (peak is the highest daily volume occuring in winter, total is the entire annual volume).
